The Analysis and Design of Algorithm

This course includes " The theoretical study of computer-program performance and resource usage ". Why study algorithms and performance? We have 6 reasons for you. 1. Algorithms help us to understand scalability. 2. Performance often draws the line between what is feasible and what is impossible. 3. Algorithmic mathematics provides a languagefor talking about program behavior. 4. Performance is the currencyof computing. 5. The lessons of program performance generalize to other computing resources 6. Speed is fun!
