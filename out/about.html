About WiLNA

Welcome to WiLNA based in School of Software, Dalian University of Technology (DUT), China. WiLNA is the abbreviation of " Wireless Lab For Network Application ". WiLNA is also a name for babys which means Friendship，Affection and Determined Protector. We borrow this word to express the idea that we become a member of WiLNA not only because of the affection of the academic research, we also have developed deep friendship and become a lovely family.

We are interested in a broad spectrum of cutting-edge research topics including Wireless Network, WiFi access control, Security and privacy in vehicular and social networks, Neutrality and ISP Network Services and Keystroke Recognition.

Feel free to contact us if you have any questions. Our email address is WiLNAlab@gmail.com.

Thank you for your interest!
